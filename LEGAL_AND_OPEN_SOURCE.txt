PROTOCOL INFORMATION & ATTRIBUTION
------------------------------------
The TCP/IP communication protocol implemented in this software is based on
publicly available documentation provided by TOA Corporation.

The full protocol specification for the M-864D Digital Stereo Mixer can be
found in the official product manual, available on the TOA Canada website:

  https://www.toacanada.com

All protocol commands, packet formats, byte values, and behavioural details
used in this software were sourced from that publicly available documentation.
